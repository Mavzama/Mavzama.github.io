Music Band Branding

In this project, I developed the rebranding for Mueres en el Mar, a Chilean-Canadian dream pop band inspired by nature. I created the visual identity for their new album Voluntad, which translates to “Will” or “Desire,” symbolizing the driving force behind life. Beyond the album cover, I also designed merchandise concepts for their upcoming tour, expanding the visual language of the rebrand. This project combined various design techniques: I used Photoshop for the album photography and effects, and Illustrator to create the album layout, merchandise assets, and logo
